About Sandip Foundation


Sandip Foundation established in 2005, affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University is the epitome of knowledge across Maharashtra and also well known for its best engineering colleges & management colleges in Nashik. We have created a benchmark in providing world-class programs to educate students of different fields. Our Institute has been ranked in the top 100 colleges in India and thereby managed to attract some of the very prestigious recruiters, and they have built a talent pipeline from Sandip Foundation to fulfil their talent requirements constantly. Our Faculties have set sublime standards for departing knowledge in terms of technical and managerial aspects in the new era of dynamic globalisation. Our Foundation has numerous colleges situated in the city of Nashik and Sijoul offering a wide range of courses in engineering, management, pharmacy and polytechnic.

Vision

At Sandip Foundation, our core Vision revolves around the proactive engagement in creating, preserving, renewing, and disseminating knowledge with the ultimate aim of contributing to the betterment of our global community. We firmly believe in the transformative power of education and its potential to shape a brighter future. Through our innovative educational approach, we empower our students to gain insights into tomorrow's challenges today, equipping them with the necessary skills and mindset to emerge as leaders of tomorrow. Central to our ethos are values of competence, conscience, and compassion, which we instill in our students to drive positive change and make a lasting impact on the world.

Values

Our institution's Values is to actively engage in the creation, preservation, renewal, and dissemination of knowledge for the betterment of our world. We are dedicated to empowering our students to learn today what will shape tomorrow, fostering their development as leaders with competence, conscience, and compassion. By instilling these values, we believe our graduates will be equipped to address the challenges of our time and contribute positively to society, making a lasting impact on the world we live in..
